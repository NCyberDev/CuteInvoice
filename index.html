🇵🇹 Portuguese Tax Information for Makeup Artists (2024) 🌸

💎 IVA (VAT) - Value Added Tax

Rate: 23% (general rate)

Threshold: Must register if turnover > €14,500 (€15,000 from 2025)

Payment: Quarterly payments (you mentioned you pay every 3 months)

Deadline: 7 days after the reporting period ends

✨ Income Tax (IRS) - 2024 Rates

Tax-free allowance: €4,104

Progressive rates:

  • Up to €7,703: 13.25%
  • €7,704–€11,623: 18%
  • €11,624–€16,472: 23%
  • €16,473–€21,131: 26%
  • And higher brackets up to 48%

💡 Recommended Tax Strategy

Set aside approximately 35-40% of each invoice to cover:

  • IVA (VAT): 23%
  • Income Tax: ~15-20% (depends on total income)
